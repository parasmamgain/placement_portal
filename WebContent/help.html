Help

1. Popular Questions
	What is Resume Display service?
	Resume Display service is designed to highlight your profile to recruiters. You get marked as a featured candidate in recruiter search,
	thereby	indicating to recruiters that you are actively looking for a job change. Moreover, your resume gets refreshed every week to make
	it appear on top in recruiters search.

2.How long does it take for my resume to get displayed on your website?
	Incase taken with Resume writing, 4 working days from the date of resume finalization
	In case not taken with Resume writing, 4 working days from the date of receipt of the updated resume and payment, whichever is later.

3.What all documents/details are required?
	For Education check we require the following details/documents-
		Name of the course
		Specialization
		Year of Passing
		Name of the institute/college
		Roll No./Registration No.
		Scanned copy of degree/certificates
		Scanned copy of marksheets
	For Employment check we require the following details/documents-
		Employers Name
		Designation
		Duration
		Annual Salary
		Employee Code
		Employers address
		Scanned copy of experience letter
		Scanned copy of offer letter
		Scanned copy of salary slip
	
4.hat all information can be verified?
	You can get the following credentials verified-
		Education (10th/12th marksheets, Graduation degrees, Post-graduation degrees/diplomas, Certification courses)
		Employment (Previous or Current)
